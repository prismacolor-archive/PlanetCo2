PlanetCo learn to budget while building your own planet!

Level One

The Galaxy Federation has awarded you 500 quatros to begin building your planet. Try to purchase as many items as you can without going over 500 quatros. If you suceed, you will move on to Level Two and win a bonus reward. Also, if you have money left over, you will receive additional points.



Tip: Buying useful things like water and plants can earn you more money in later levels!
