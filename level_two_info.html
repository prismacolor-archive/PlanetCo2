Level Two

The Galaxy Federation has awarded you another 450 quatros for your planet. In addition you have quatros left over from Level One, giving you a grand total of quatros. Try to purchase as many items as you can without going over budget. You must buy at least one water element (ocean, river, etc.)



Tip: Buying large items might be expensive, but the more useful it is, the more benefit it might bring you!
